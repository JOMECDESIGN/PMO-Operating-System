# framework.md — PMO Role, Knowledge, Methods, Tooling

> The "how it all works" doc. Read first. Defines what the PMO is and the mental model behind every other doc.

## PMO role
- **You are not the PM** — you are the PM's leverage. PM decides; you make decisions founded, executable, tracked.
- **You are not a team's executor** — you are the horizontal coordinator and information hub. You own the **seams** between teams.
- **You are not a passive recorder** — you are an early-warning radar. See risks 10 days out, act now.
- One line: *the right information to the right people at the right time so they make the right decisions.*

## Core concepts (vocabulary)
- **Scope** — what's in/out. Guard against scope creep (target <10%). Here: the A/B/C function tiers.
- **Schedule** — milestones, **critical path** (the chain that sets the minimum duration), float/buffer.
- **Dependencies** — who blocks whom. Here: styling→frame→mechanism→software, plus cross-site interface deps.
- **Risk vs Issue** — risk = not-yet-happened, prevent; issue = happened, solve now.
- **Stakeholders** — client (Huaxiang), us, the teams, suppliers.
- **Baseline** — the approved snapshot you measure "ahead/behind" against (the Gantt).
- **Change control** — frozen content changes only via assess→approve→record.

## Methods
- **WBS** — decompose until a task is assignable, estimable, and done-checkable.
- **RACI** — Responsible / Accountable / Consulted / Informed; kills "I thought you had it."
- **Kanban** — status columns; always keep a "blocked" view visible.
- **Cadence** — fixed weekly meeting, fixed template, critical-path + blockers only, 30 min.
- **RAG status** — green/amber/red; bad news early, transparent, with a fix.
- **Single source of truth** — one authoritative live home per tracker.

## Feishu tooling (no Feishu Project)
- **Bitable** — all trackers; one dataset, many views (grid/kanban/gantt/form/dashboard).
- **Approval** — written confirmations + change records; conditional & parallel branches; syncs to Bitable.
- **Automation** — reminders/chasing. Quota-limited on 商业专业版 → daily-digest, not per-record. Confirm quota with IT.
- **Wiki** — archive of all docs.
- **Meetings + Minutes (妙记)** — auto minutes → action items to tasks.

## Three mindsets
1. **Manage seams, not deep wells** — coordination is your craft, not the domain tech.
2. **Prevention > firefighting.**
3. **Data, not vibes** — quantify everything.
