## Milestone health (RAG)
- 🟢 on track, prerequisites ready, expected on time.
- 🟡 at-risk but controllable — prerequisite slightly late / buffer consumed; act.
- 🔴 off-track — prerequisite badly late or gate unmet; likely to slip; intervene/escalate.

## Weekly report — 6 sections
1. **Overall health** (RAG one-liner; bad news with a fix).
2. **Milestones** (table with health + verdict).
3. **Critical path** (which tasks, status, buffer consumed in days).
4. **Risk alerts** (triggered/monitoring risks + actions).
5. **This week done / next week planned** (against the plan).
6. **Decisions needed** (who must decide, what, by when).

## Overall-health rule
**Overall = the worst component.** Any red milestone, any triggered high risk, or exhausted critical-path buffer → overall is red. Honest amber/red beats cosmetic green.

## Two iron laws
1. Bad news early, transparent, **with a proposed fix** — red-only reports have little value.
2. **Data, not vibes** — "planned 5, delivered 2, buffer down 3 days," not "seems slow."

## Weekly flow (30 min)
Before: update the three trackers + Gantt. In meeting: critical path + triggered/near risks + near milestones only. After: assemble report from trackers, publish to Wiki, CC client. Optional: 商业专业版 dashboard AI summary can draft the report; PMO reviews/polishes.
